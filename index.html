About

Ali grew up in Manila where she learned patience by sitting in traffic jams and survived socializing with passable Tagalog. She graduated with a Computer Science degree from Ateneo de Manila University, where she had begun her dual life of juggling tech by day and art in her free time. She now lives in Tokyo where she works as a systems engineer and moonlights as a visual artist.
